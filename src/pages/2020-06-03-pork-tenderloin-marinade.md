tags:

category: Dinner
summary: Soy-asian marinade for a pork tenderloin.
ingredients:
  - 1/4 cup brown sugar
  - 1/2 tsp ginger
  - 2 cloves chopped garlic
  - 1/2 cup soy sauce
  - 1/4 cup water
  - 1/2 tsp pepper
  - 2 Tbsp oil
path: /asianporkmarinade/
---
Mix ingredients in a bag with pork tenderloin. Refrigerate for a few hours, preferably over night.
